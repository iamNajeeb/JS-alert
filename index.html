About By Allama khadin Hussain Rizvi

His full name is Allama khadin Hussain Rizvi.He was a very handsome and beatiful person. He was a true lover of Rasool S.A.W. He used to teach Friday in Makkah Masjid,Lahore.He was a serious religious scholar and Sheikh of Hadith.He was also a Hafiz of Allama Iqbal's poetry. He died in 2020 in the city of Lahore.His funeral was conducted by his son(who later became the Ameer of the Moverment).His Mazar-e- Shareef was built in Jamia Masjid Rehmat-ul-Lalameen.
